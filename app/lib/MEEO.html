About MEEO (www.meeo.it)

Founded in 2004 in Ferrara, Italy, MEEO started its activity providing products and services for atmospheric pollution monitoring, extending successively its application domain to the Earth surface mapping. Since 2006 MEEO is a consolidated partner of the European Space Agency and provides its services to public and private entities.

 

Coverages info

General information on the production of air quality forecast and analysis over Europe. The MACC regional air quality (http://macc-raq.gmes-atmosphere.eu) production is based on seven state-of-the-art numerical air quality models developed in Europe: CHIMERE by INERIS (France), EMEP by MET Norway (Norway), EURAD-IM by University of Cologne (Germany), LOTOS-EUROS by KNMI (Netherlands), MATCH by SMHI (Sweden), MOCAGE by Météo-France (France) and SILAM at FMI(Finland). The seven model forecasts and analyses are combined via an ensemble approach, that provides on average better estimates of air pollutant concentrations than the individual models.

 

Products

The Climate Data Service provides MACC regional air quality forecasts over Europe (30°N - 70°N, 25°W - 45°E): ozone (O3), nitrogen dioxide (NO2), sulphur dioxide (SO2), carbon monoxide (CO), particulate matters (PM10 and PM2.5) at surface, at 500m, 1000m and 3000m altitude, on an hourly basis.
