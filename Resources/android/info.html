Info

This app allows to:

  • 1) Access a specific Climate Data service that, according to the position of the device, detected by its GPS sensor, connects to a WCS server operated by a MEEO and retrieve/display meteorological and pollution data;
  • 2) Use a general-purpose service to connect to different WCS/WMS servers, explore their capabilities and download/display/overlay coverages according to a given bounding box;
  • 3) Access a geographically distributed repository of a sample of ESA MERIS multi-format data stored both on local and Grid/Cloud resources.
